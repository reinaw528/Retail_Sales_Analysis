# Retail Sales Analysis
## Project overview
This project analyzes retail sales performance across different regions, customer segments, and product categories.
The goal is to identify sales trends, evaluate profitability, and provide actionable business recommendations.
## Business problem
The company wants to understand sales trends, profitablility, and customer performance.
## Executive Summary

This project analyzes retail sales performance across regions, customer segments, and product categories using Python, SQL, and Power BI.

Key findings include:

- California generated the highest sales revenue.
- Technology was the top-performing product category.
- Consumer customers contributed the largest share of sales.
- Revenue showed consistent growth over the analyzed period.
- Customer revenue was well diversified, with no single customer contributing more than 5% of total sales.

Based on these findings, the business should continue investing in high-performing regions and technology products while maintaining a diversified customer base.
## Dashboard 1 - Sales Performance Overview

## Dashboard 3 - Sales Performance Overview
Purpose: Identify high-value customers and top-performing products.
Key Insights:
- Revenue is highly diversified across customers.
- Top 10 customers contributed only 6.83% of total sales.
